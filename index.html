Free, and meant to stay free

This kind of tool has sat behind big-money subscriptions and per-view quotas for too long — pricing artists out of their own work. Viewall ARt is built to be owned and shared: no click quotas, no locked exports. Made for community and open development.

How it works

  1. In the Builder, load the image you want to trigger from and lay out your elements.
  2. Set what each element does — play, animate, open a link.
  3. Hit Publish to get a self-contained package you can host anywhere.
  4. Anyone points a phone at the print through the Viewer — and it comes alive.
